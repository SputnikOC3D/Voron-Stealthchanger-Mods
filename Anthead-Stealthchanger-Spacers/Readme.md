# Anthead Stealthchanger Spacers

Spacer components designed to enhance the Anthead Stealthchanger backplate mounting system.

## Overview

Create a more user-friendly wire channel in two different directions. The parts are mirrored to reflect channels for different possible extruders.

Additionally, the feet or legs extend the full length of the Anthead hotend mount in the Anthead/Stealthchanger backplate interface, providing a more stable mounting surface to the back plate.

## Features

- Mirrored design for left and right configurations
- Enhanced wire management and channel routing
- Extended feet for improved stability
- Compatible with Anthead/Stealthchanger systems
